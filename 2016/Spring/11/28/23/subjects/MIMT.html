DESCRIPTIONConsumers react to marketing information provided about a product. The topics discussed comprise an examination of basic psychological processes in a logical sequence: i.e., exposure, attention, perception, memory, persuasion, and decision-making. Advertising - and how consumers process ads - is the major vehicle used to clarify these concepts. Thus, this course both a micro and macro perspective on the factors influencing a customer.
