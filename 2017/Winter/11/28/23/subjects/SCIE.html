DESCRIPTIONThe course is offered in two consecutive terms, such as Fall to Winter, Winter to Spring, or Spring to Summer. The course is in a form of a community project at a designated non-governmental organization (NGO) under the supervision of the School. Each project will consist of at least four elements, namely training workshops delivered by members at the host NGO, design, implementation and execution of the service-project as instructed by the host NGO. Course Coordinator's approval is required for taking the course. Students may not repeat the course for credits. Graded P or F. Maybe graded PP.
