DESCRIPTIONThis is a course which develops the rigorous definition of real and complex numbers, and various aspects of polynomials.
INTENDED
LEARNING
OUTCOMES		On successful completion of the course, students will be able to:
1.Use Peano axioms to define natural numbers and their arithmetic rigorously.
2.Use the method of Dedekind cuts to rigorously define the field of real numbers.
3.Define complex numbers and apply them to algebraic and geometric problems.
4.Describe the connection between polynomials and geometric objects defined by them.  
5.Work with various special polynomials and show a variety of their applications.
